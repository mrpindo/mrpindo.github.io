ICT For Development

InfoPrima is a lean company with personnel who have years of experience in real ICT4D software solution development.

ICT4D usually associated with applications in developing countries. It is concerned with directly applying information technology approaches to poverty reduction. ICTs can be applied directly, benefiting the disadvantaged population, or indirectly, by assisting aid organizations, non-governmental organizations, governments, and/or businesses, to improve socio-economic conditions.

Our service will benefit government projects, profit/non-profit organizations and academic institutions, as well as small to medium size companies.
